evok.es (verb)

definition: (1) brings or recalls to the conscious mind. (2) elicits or calls forth.

latin root of evoke evocare

e out of, from + vocare to call

evoke vs. invoke

Evoke means to draw forth or to call something to mind and usually applies to feelings and memories. It starts with an “E” and is Effortless because it is less purposefully active. Invoke has a few different meanings such as to call on, to appeal to, and to call for. (source)
